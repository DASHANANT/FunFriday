Two Truths and a Lie

"Two Truths and a Lie" is a fun and interactive online game where players take turns sharing two true statements and one false statement about themselves. The challenge is to identify the lie among the truths.

How to Play

  1. Step 1: Gather a group of players to participate in the game.
  2. Step 2: Each player takes turns sharing two true statements and one false statement about themselves in any order.
  3. Step 3: Other players listen to the statements and try to guess which statement is the lie.
  4. Step 4: After all guesses are made, the player reveals the lie.
  5. Step 5: Points can be awarded to players who correctly identify the lie.

Requirements

  • An online platform for hosting virtual game sessions (video conferencing, messaging apps, etc.).
  • A group of players to participate in the game.

Time Requirement

The game can vary in duration based on the number of players and the pace of sharing and guessing statements.
