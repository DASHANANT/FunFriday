Escape Room Challenge

"Escape Room Challenge" is an exciting offline team-based game where players work together to solve puzzles, find clues, and escape from a themed room within a set time limit.

How to Play

  1. Step 1: Gather a team of players (usually 2-8 participants).
  2. Step 2: Choose an available themed room to challenge yourselves with.
  3. Step 3: Enter the room and start exploring to find hidden clues and puzzles.
  4. Step 4: Collaborate as a team to solve the puzzles and unlock new areas.
  5. Step 5: Use the clues you find to progress and ultimately escape the room within the given time limit (usually 60 minutes).

Requirements

  • A group of 2-8 players to form a team.
  • An available escape room facility with various themed rooms.

Time Requirement

Each game session typically lasts around 60 minutes.
