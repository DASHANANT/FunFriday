How to Play Snakes and Ladders

Snakes and Ladders is a classic board game. Here's how to play:

  1. Choose the number of players from the dropdown menu.
  2. Select the board size (8x8 or 10x10).
  3. Click the "Start" button to begin the game.
  4. Roll the dice to move your pawn.
  5. Follow the ladders up and snakes down.
  6. The first player to reach the end wins!

Have fun playing!
