UNO Cards

  • Category: Board Game
  • Play: Start Playing

Rules

UNO is a popular card game played with a deck of special cards.
The objective is to be the first player to get rid of all your cards.
Players take turns matching a card from their hand with the top card of the discard pile by color or number.
Special action cards add twists and turns to the game.
Plan your moves strategically, and don't forget to shout "UNO" when you're down to one card!
The first player to play all their cards wins the round, and points are tallied based on opponents' remaining cards.

Additional Information

  • Players: 2 to 10
  • Age: 7 and up
  • Play Time: 15-30 minutes

Word Whiz

  • Category: Board Game
  • Play: Start Playing

Rules

Test your vocabulary and word-building skills.
Create words using letter tiles on the game board.
Earn points based on the complexity and length of words.
Compete with fellow word enthusiasts in a friendly challenge.
Unleash your inner wordsmith and have fun with language!
