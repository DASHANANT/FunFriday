Charades

  • Category: Online Game
  • Play: Start Playing

Rules

One player acts out a word or phrase without speaking.
Others guess what the word or phrase is based on the actions.
Use a timer to limit each round of acting and guessing.
Rotate the acting player after each round.
Have fun and let your creativity shine!

Pictionary

  • Category: Online Game
  • Play: Start Playing

Rules

One player draws a word or phrase while others guess what it is.
Use an online whiteboard or drawing tool to sketch.
Set a time limit for drawing and guessing.
Rotate roles so everyone gets a chance to draw and guess.
Let your artistic side shine and have a blast!

Guess The Word

  • Category: Online Game
  • Play: Start Playing

Rules

One player thinks of a word and keeps it secret.
Players take turns guessing the word based on provided clues.
Set a limit on the number of guesses or play until someone gets it right.
Rotate roles after each round.
Have fun and enjoy the game!
