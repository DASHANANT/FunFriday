Instructions

Human Knot

  • Category: Offline Team Fun Game
  • Play: In-person

Instructions

Gather your team in a circle and have everyone close their eyes.
Everyone extends both hands into the circle and grabs the hands of two different people.
Open your eyes and work together to untangle the human knot without letting go of hands.
Communicate, strategize, and have a good laugh as you navigate the challenge.
Build trust and cooperation among team members!

PowerPoint Karaoke

  • Category: Presentation Game
  • Play: In-person or Online

Instructions

Prepare a set of random PowerPoint slides with diverse topics.
Participants are given a random slide they've never seen before and must present it.
They need to improvise and give a convincing presentation, even if it doesn't make sense!
Use a timer to keep presentations short and rotate presenters.
Have a good time with hilarious and creative impromptu presentations!
